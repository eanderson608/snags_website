Snag a cold one at Snag’s Sports Bar!

Located on Highway 63 in Spooner, Wisconsin off of the Wild River ATV and Snowmobile Trail and next to the Best Western, Snag’s Sports Bar offers a fun and friendly environment to watch the game or just have a few drinks. We open every day at noon (sometimes earlier for Packer and Badger games) and Happy Hour is from noon to six Monday through Thursday and noon to five on Friday. Stop in today!
